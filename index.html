About Us

Friends of the Universe Climate Action Adaptation Society FOUCCAS is a registered organization, established on July 17, 2024, and recognized by the Ministry of Environment, Climate Change, and Forestry. It is dedicated to tackling climate change and global warming, aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UNSDGs) and the Kenyan government's agenda. The organization focuses on forest restoration, ecosystem protection, and supporting the planting of 15 billion trees in Kenya by 2032 and beyond. FOUCCAS collaborates with partners to create practical solutions and strategies to help people and the planet adapt to climate change impacts.

OUR MISSION

Our misson is to create a MICE platform for capacity building, innovation incubation, and development to promote and enhance practical adaptation solutions that help people and the planet cope with the effects of climate change and global warming. We recognize and acknowledge the role of public-private partnerships (PPP) as outlined in the bottom-up transformation agenda and consistent with UNSDG Goal 17. We are committed to working with the government, NGOs, the private sector, and the international community to offer solutions on climate change and global warming.

Thematic Areas of Engagement include and not limited to:

  • Air Quality
  • Critical Infrastructure
  • Ecosystem Protection
  • Wildlife Protection
  • Energy
  • Food and Agriculture
  • Policies and Planning
  • Public Health
  • Solid Waste Management
  • Transportation
  • Water Management

Our Vision

1

To enhance capacity building on climate change advocacy and develop climate resilient solutions through training, innovation incubation and development for sustainable development.

2

Develop youth and women empowerment programs through smart farming and food security initiatives across the country.

3

Development of tree nurseries to give seedlings for both indigenous trees and orchard (fruit tree) to support the forest cover restoration and empowerment around the 47 counties in Kenya.
